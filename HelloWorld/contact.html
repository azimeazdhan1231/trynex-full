Frequently Asked Questions

How long does custom printing take?

Custom printing typically takes 2-3 business days for production, plus shipping time. Rush orders can be accommodated for an additional fee.

What file formats do you accept for custom designs?

We accept JPG, PNG, PDF, and AI files. For best quality, we recommend high-resolution images (300 DPI) in PNG or PDF format.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer attractive discounts for bulk orders. Contact us with your requirements for a custom quote.

What is your return policy?

We offer returns for defective products within 7 days of delivery. Custom items are not returnable unless there's a production error.

Do you deliver outside Dhaka?

Yes, we deliver nationwide across Bangladesh. Delivery times and charges may vary based on location.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! You'll receive an order ID that you can use to track your order status on our website.
